NOSY TANY KELY

description:

Nosy Tanikely and its marine park awaits you !
The site is known world-wide for its exceptional biodiversity,
find yourself enchanted by the place’s beauty.
Dive into a translucent water where all kind of fishes swim among corals.
It is a trip to enjoy in family and introduce yourselves to the joys of diving.
Nosy Antanihely, “the island of the small earth” or Nosy Tanikely acquired several years ago the status of Marine and Coastal Protected Area .
The park’s management is entrusted to the “Tanihely National Park” association composed of three institutional entities among which Madagascar National Park.
Despite its small size, Nosy Tanikely is recognized for its remarkable marine and land biodiversity.
Scientific studies certify that the site is one of Madagascar’s most important in terms of marine characteristics,
the park is thus visited by many holidaymaker every year. In the agenda : sunbathing, diving/snorkeling, picnics and walks toward the island’s peak.
